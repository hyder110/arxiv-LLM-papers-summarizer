Summary:
This paper compares the performance of three large language models (LLMs) - OpenAI ChatGPT, Microsoft Bing Chat, and Google Bard - on the VNHSGE English dataset. The results show that BingChat performs better than ChatGPT and Bard, indicating that BingChat and Bard can replace ChatGPT in Vietnam. The LLMs also outperform Vietnamese students in English language proficiency, suggesting their potential as effective tools for teaching and learning English at the high school level. The paper provides insights into the potential and limitations of LLMs in the context of Vietnamese education.
